About The Event

About The Event

Space Biology Horizons: Shaping Catalunya's Future in Space brings together experts to explore the transformative potential of space biology. The event highlights groundbreaking research, fosters collaboration, and provides actionable guidance on accessing space for research, funding opportunities, and entrepreneurship to drive innovation and regional partnerships in the field.
